About Us

We are FTC Team 31742 - SuperNOVA, proudly powered by FRC Team 3255 - The SuperNURDs. (San Pasqual high school) Our program introduces middle school students to the exciting world of FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), where we design, build, and program robots to tackle unique challenges. Along the way, we learn collaboration, problem-solving, and creativity skills that go far beyond robotics.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate and curious students from Bear Valley Middle School, guided and mentored by dedicated high school volunteers from San Pasqual's FRC Team 3255 SuperNURD's. With their support and mentorship, we learn how to transform bold ideas into real-world solutions, one robot at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire future innovators and leaders by empowering students to grow in STEM, teamwork, and leadership. Through robotics, we aim to spark curiosity, build confidence, and prepare ourselves to shape the technology of tomorrow.
