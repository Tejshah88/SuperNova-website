Who We Are

We are FTC Team 31742, SuperNOVA. We are a team of passionate and curious students from Bear Valley Middle School, guided and mentored by dedicated high school volunteers from San Pasqual's FRC Team 3255, the SuperNURDs.

With their support, we transform bold ideas into real-world solutions, one robot at a time. Our program joins the exciting world of FIRST Tech Challenge, where students design, build, and program robots to tackle unique seasonal challenges.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire future innovators and leaders by empowering students to grow in STEM, teamwork, and leadership.

Through robotics, we aim to spark curiosity, build confidence, and prepare ourselves to shape the technology of tomorrow. Along the way we develop collaboration, problem-solving, and creativity skills that go far beyond the robot.

FIRST Tech Challenge

FTC is a competition for students in grades 7 through 12 where teams design and build robots to play a game that changes every year. Teams also produce engineering notebooks, develop outreach programs, and present their work to judges.

Our Community

Proudly rooted in Escondido, CA. We partner with the SuperNURDs FRC program to give middle schoolers access to expert mentorship, tools, and facilities that allow us to compete at the highest level in San Diego FTC competition.
