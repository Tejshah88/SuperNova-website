About Us

We are FTC Team 31742 - SuperNOVA, proudly powered by FRC Team 3255 - The SuperNURDs. (San Pasqual high school) Our program introduces middle school students to the exciting world of FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), where we design, build, and program robots to tackle unique challenges. Along the way, we learn collaboration, problem-solving, and creativity skills that go far beyond robotics.
